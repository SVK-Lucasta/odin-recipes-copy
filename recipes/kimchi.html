Kimchi Pancake

Description

Kimchi Pancakes are one of the quickest yet easiest ways to use a jar of Kimchi! Made with only a few ingredients, it's perfect for a busy night.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 pound kimchi, chopped in small pieces
  • 1/2 teaspoon sugar
  • 1/2 cup flour
  • 1/2 cup water
  • oil or cooking spray

Steps

  1. Combine kimchi, kimchi brine, sugar, flour and water in a medium bowl and mix well with a spoon
  2. Heat a skillet over medium heat
  3. Add oil and swirl to coat
  4. Pour batter into pan to make a large cirlce
  5. Cook until bottom is golden-brown, 3-5 minutes
  6. Turn the pancake over
  7. Cook again. 3-5 minutes
  8. Serve with sauce of choice
