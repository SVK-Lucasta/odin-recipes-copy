Yam Fries

Description

A healthy and delicious reciple with minimal ingredients! Perfect for getting a daily dose of veggies in.

Ingredients

  • 1 sweet potato
  • salt
  • garlic powder
  • paprika
  • black pepper
  • oil or cooking spray

Steps

  1. Preheat air fryer to 380F. Peel sweet potatoes then slice into even 1/4 inch sticks
  2. Toss with garlic powder, paprika, salt and black pepper
  3. Cook in 2-3 batches depending on the size of air fryer basket without overcrowding. 10 minutes recommended, turning half way.
  4. Serve with dipping sauce of choice
